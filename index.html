About Me

I’m Prabin Dhital, a mechanical engineer passionate about tackling complex challenges and crafting elegant, manufacturable solutions. With a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering and over a decade of experience across off-highway vehicles, electronic enclosures, and cable installation tools, I transform concepts into production-ready products.

As a Senior Design Engineer at Caterpillar Inc., I lead rubber component development and validation, manage lab operations, and collaborate with global teams to deliver high-performance results. I hold EIT and PMP certifications, and I continuously expand my skills in additive manufacturing, statistical analysis, and DFMEA. Outside work, I mentor emerging engineers and support STEM outreach as an Industrial Advisor.
